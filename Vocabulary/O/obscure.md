## 解釋：
v. 使模糊，使難理解的
to make sth. difficult to understand or know.

adj. 模糊的，費解的
Not readily understand or clearly expressed.

adj. 默默無聞的
Not prominent or famous.


諧音：
ob(負向)secure(獲取)
無法獲取，所以難以理解
